About People's Agronomy

Let's know more about cultivation in our own cities

Most of elderly people want to do some productive work with their interests in it.So cultivating crops near their homes is a dream for many of them. Especially in cities it's nearly impossible. But with our application we can actually make their dream come true with many other extra benefits. We unite people of same town and also allow them to choose the area that they want to grow crops and allot them their wished area.

The modern generation is compressed between work,aims,jobs and many other stressful works.So their mental health is being effecting due to lack of time and their physical health is being effected due to lack of organic food and excercise.So people should start doing activities for their refreshment which is very important for both of their mental and physical health.

We have many objectives in buiding a perfect Community Garden.

FEATURES:

  • In cities we have many parks, in the same way instead of parks we will have these town agricutural sites for the people to combine and grow crops.
  • The people of same community will be formed into groups according to their interest in which crops are to be cultivated.
  • We also send remainders for the user's mobile, to look after the cultivation land time to time.
  • Also chat rooms will be created for the people of same community to share their interests or about the next crops they want to grow.

These features not only help people to grow organic plants but also it phsychologically helps the people to come out from their sufferings and make them into an optimistic human.
